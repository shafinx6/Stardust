The Commander

Yuriy Stepanov

Yuiry Stepanov is a Russian engineer, former Marine Corps pilot and former NASA astronaut. He launched into space for the third time as commander of Crew Dragon Demo-2. Coming back for his fourth time with Stardust for mission LIGHTSPEED.

The Pilot

Kahlil Garner

Pilot on the first operational flight of the Stardust Crew Dragon to the International Space Station. Garner is a commander in the U.S. Navy where he pilots an F/A-18.He was a crew member of Expedition 64, and served as a station systems flight engineer.

The Engineer

Amina Iris

Amina Iris is an Iranian American engineer and co-founder of Stardust Communications. Amina was the fourth self-funded space tourist, the first self-funded woman to fly to the ISS, and the first Iranian in space.
